Maswali Yanayoulizwa Mara kwa Mara Frequently Asked Questions

Je, huduma yenu ni ya bure?

Is your service free?

Ndiyo, kukusanya taka za kielektroniki ni bure kwa wakazi wote wa Zanzibar. Hatutozii gharama yoyote ya ukusanyaji.

Yes, e-waste collection is free for all Zanzibar residents. We do not charge any collection fees.

Je, mnachukua taka aina gani?

What types of waste do you collect?

Tunachukua vifaa vyote vya kielektroniki: simu, laptop, TV, printer, betri, na zaidi. Pia tunachukua vipuri na vifaa vya zamani.

We collect all electronic devices: phones, laptops, TVs, printers, batteries, and more. We also take spare parts and old equipment.

Je, mnatoa bei kwa vifaa vinavyofanya kazi?

Do you pay for working devices?

Ndiyo, tunanunua vifaa vinavyofanya kazi na vya vipuri kwa bei nzuri. Tunakagua kifaa na kutoa ofa kabla ya kununua.

Yes, we buy working devices and spare parts at good prices. We inspect the device and make an offer before purchasing.

Je, mnachukua taka kwa siku ngapi baada ya kuripoti?

How many days after reporting do you collect?

Tunachukua taka ndani ya siku 2-3 za kazi baada ya kuripoti. Utapokea simu kuthibitisha siku na muda wa kuchukua.

We collect waste within 2-3 working days after reporting. You will receive a call to confirm the collection day and time.

Je, ninaweza kuwasilisha taka wenyewe?

Can I drop off waste myself?

Ndiyo, unaweza kuleta taka zako kwenye kituo chetu kilichopo Stone Town, Jumatatu hadi Ijumaa, saa 8:00 asubuhi hadi 5:00 mchana.

Yes, you can drop off your waste at our center in Stone Town, Monday to Friday, 8:00 am to 5:00 pm.

Je, mnatoa cheti cha uchakataji?

Do you provide recycling certificates?

Ndiyo, tunatoa cheti cha uchakataji kwa biashara na taasisi zinazohitaji uthibitisho wa utupaji wa taka kwa njia sahihi.

Yes, we provide recycling certificates for businesses and institutions that need proof of proper waste disposal.
