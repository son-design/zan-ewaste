HAMIDU IBRAHIMU NCHALIKWAO

Founder & CEO
Kugeuza taka kuwa fursa kwa Zanzibar safi Turning waste into opportunity for a cleaner Zanzibar

Historia Yangu My Story

HAMIDU IBRAHIMU NCHALIKWAO ni mjasiriamali wa kijamii na mtaalamu wa Kompyuta na mazingira aliyejitolea kutatua changamoto ya taka za kielektroniki Zanzibar. Akiwa na asili ya teknolojia na upendo wa mazingira, alianzisha ZanEwaste Hub mwaka 2025.

HAMIDU IBRAHIMU NCHALIKWAO is a Digital entrepreneur Computer expert and environmental dedicated to solving the e-waste challenge in Zanzibar. With a background in technology and a love for the environment, he founded ZanEwaste Hub in 2025.

Leo, kupitia kazi yake na timu yake, wameweza kukusanya zaidi ya tani 5 za taka na kuajiri vijana 25 kama wakusanyaji na mafundi.

Today, through his work and team, they have collected over 5 tons of waste and employed 25 youth as collectors and technicians.
