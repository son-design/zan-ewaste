Sisi ni Nani? Who We Are?

ZanEwaste Hub ni mfumo wa kwanza wa kidijitali wa usimamizi wa taka za kielektroniki Zanzibar. Tumejitolea kusaidia jamii kuchakata taka kwa njia rafiki kwa mazingira.

ZanEwaste Hub is the first digital e-waste management system in Zanzibar. We are committed to helping the community recycle waste in an environmentally friendly way.

Tumeanza Kama wazo na tunakusudia kukusanya zaidi ya tani 5 za taka za kielektroniki na kuajiri vijana kama wakusanyaji Wabunifu na mafundi.

We started as an idea and we are aim to collect over 5 tons of e-waste and employed youth as collectors , innovators and technicians.

Dhamira Yetu Our Mission

Kuwawezesha Wazanzibari wote kuchakata taka za kielektroniki kwa uwajibikaji kupitia mfumo rahisi wa kidijitali. Tunakusanya kutoka wilaya zote za Unguja na Pemba.

Empower all Zanzibaris to responsibly recycle e-waste through a simple digital system. We collect from all districts of Unguja and Pemba.

Maono Yetu Our Vision

Kutokomeza taka za kielektroniki Zanzibar ifikapo 2030 - kugeuza taka kuwa rasilimali mpya na kuchochea uchumi wa mzunguko.

Eliminate e-waste in Zanzibar by 2030 - turning waste into new resources and promoting a circular economy.
